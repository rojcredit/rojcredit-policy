Data Deletion Policy for RojCredit

Effective Date: November 2025

At RojCredit, we value your privacy and provide you full control over your personal and business data. This Data Deletion Policy explains how you can request deletion of your account and associated data stored in our systems.

1. Data We Store

When you use RojCredit, we securely store limited personal and business information such as:

  • Your mobile number (for login and authentication)
  • Business profile details (business name, type, address, GST, etc.)
  • Customer and transaction data that you choose to record in the app

All data is stored in a secure cloud database (Google Firebase). We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

2. Requesting Data Deletion

You may delete your RojCredit account and all related information at any time.

To request account deletion:

  • Send an email to rojcredit.help@gmail.com with the subject line “Delete My Account”.
  • Include your registered mobile number and, if possible, screenshots or details to help us identify your account.
  • We will verify your identity using an OTP sent to your registered number or email before processing the request.

3. Data Removal Process

Once verified, we will permanently delete your data from our systems within 7–14 business days. This includes:

  • Your business profile information
  • All customer records and transactions
  • Any associated files or uploaded media

After deletion, the data cannot be recovered. We may retain limited information only if required by applicable laws (for example, for fraud prevention or accounting compliance).
