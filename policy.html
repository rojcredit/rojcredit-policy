RojCredit – Privacy, Data Deletion & Support Policy

Effective Date: November 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to RojCredit — a business ledger and digital accounting app developed by Ritesh Kumar. This document explains how we handle your data, your rights to delete it, and how you can reach us for any help or concerns.

2. Information We Collect

When you use RojCredit, we may collect limited personal and business-related data such as:

  • Your mobile number (for login and verification)
  • Business name, type, and contact information
  • Customer names and transaction details you manually enter

We do not collect sensitive information like your bank login, card details, or personal identification numbers.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To verify your account and keep you signed in
  • To store and sync your business and customer data securely on cloud (Firebase)
  • To improve user experience and app performance

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with any third-party company.

4. Data Security

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase using industry-standard encryption and access control. Only you can access your account information. We continuously monitor our systems to prevent unauthorized access or misuse.

5. Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account is active. If you stop using RojCredit, your data may remain securely stored until you request deletion (explained below).

6. Data Deletion Policy

You have the right to delete your account and all related data at any time.

To request account deletion:

  • Send an email to rojcredit.help@gmail.com with subject “Delete My Account”.
  • Include your registered mobile number for verification.

Once verified, your account and all data (business, customers, and transactions) will be permanently deleted from our servers within 7–14 business days. After deletion, recovery is not possible.

7. Cookies & Tracking

RojCredit does not use tracking cookies or analytics scripts to monitor personal user behavior. Only minimal usage data (crash logs and app performance data) may be collected by Firebase for technical improvements.

8. Contact & Support

If you face any issues or wish to contact our support team, reach us at:

  • App Name: RojCredit
  • Developer: Ritesh Kumar
  • Email: rojcredit.help@gmail.com
  • Support Hours: Monday – Saturday, 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM (IST)

We usually respond within 24–48 hours on working days.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically to comply with Google Play requirements or improve transparency. Updates will be reflected on this page with a new effective date. Continued use of the app after updates implies your acceptance of the revised terms.
